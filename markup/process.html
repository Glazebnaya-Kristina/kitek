Our process

01. Requirements stage

During the requirements stage, we perform a project analysis and consultation to define what the client needs our help with. We’ll sit down with the client and do some requirement gathering and discuss the project budget.

The budget will give our staff a good idea on what we can and can’t do in regards to staying within this budget. The final step of the stage is drawing up a contract that outlines our services and the cost for the client to accept and sign. Once they accept and sign the contract, we can move to the second stage of our process

Requirement
  • Project Analysis & Consultation
  • Requirement Gathering
  • Project Budget
  • Contract Acceptance

Stage 01

02. Planning stage

The planning stage is where our professional and experienced team starts to put together a plan to accomplish all of the outlined project requirements we put together in step one. We’ll do in-depth resource planning and project timeline planning so we can give an estimate on how long the project will take from start to the implementation.

This is also the stage where we outline the functional specifications of the project and do thorough documentation of every step of the specifications, research and timeline planning to ensure that we stay on-target for time and on-task for what we’re accomplishing for our client.

Planning
  • Resource Planning
  • Project Timeline Planning
  • Functional Specification
  • Documentation

Stage 02

03. Prototyping stage

The prototyping stage is where we take everything we gathered from the requirement and planning step and design a prototype around it. Our goal is to streamline the user experience and make it as simple and straightforward as possible. We design the prototype and review it with the customer.

Our staff incorporate HTML, front-end and UI/UX testing during prototyping. These things allow us to build a solid foundation to create the actual program that we tailor to our clients wants, needs and specifications.

Prototyping
  • User Experience
  • Design
  • HTML
  • Front-end
  • UI/UX Testing

Stage 03

04. Development stage

Once we have a prototype that meets all of our clients’ requirements and a prototype that does everything we need it to do, we move to the development stage. Our team will start on the source code and back-end work of the program, and we’ll also begin building the database for your program.

This is also the stage where we implement third-party integration and deploy the staging server for the project in question. We’ll also set up and execute several unit tests during this process to help ensure that the final project runs smoothly, and that the transition to it goes as easily as possible for all of the parties.

Requirement
  • Back-end
  • Source Code
  • Datbase
  • Third Party Integrations
  • Deployment on Staging Server
  • Unit Tests

Stage 04

05. Relearn and evolution stage

The final step in our five-step process is the relearn and evolution stage. This is the final step where our staff can change requests for the finished product. It’s also the step where we perform the final delivery of the program. Once we deliver it, we’ll deploy the live server as well.

We’ll start the client training process and provide meticulous documentation on how the new service works and what the client has to do to run it accordingly. Additionally, our staff are also happy to provide routine maintenance and ongoing support once the new process is fully integrated into the clients’ existing operation.

Releare & evolution

  • Change Requests
  • Final Delivery
  • Deployment on Live Server
  • Client Training & Documentation
  • Maintenance & Support
